What can I get from Hack Club?

In the first meeting, everyone will get some free stickers, and we will discuss our upcoming projects and events.

What is Hack Club?

Hack Club is a global community of teen hackers from around the world who code together
Hackers are people who build things with code – the creators, makers, and coders of tomorrow - think life hacks.
We don’t hack into your bank account or the government. We’re creators.
