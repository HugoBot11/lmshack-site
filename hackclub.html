About Hack Club

What is Hack Club?

Hack Club is an organisation founded in 2014 by Zach Latta and Jonathan Leung to encourage young people to learn and get involved with technology.

In 2016, The Hack Foundation was formed as a non-profit organisation to provide funding to Hack Clubs worldwide. HCB is a fiscal sponsorship program for non-profits around the world.

What is a hacker?

Hackers are people who build things with code – the creators, makers, and coders of tomorrow - think life hacks.
They don’t hack into your bank account or the government. They're creators.
